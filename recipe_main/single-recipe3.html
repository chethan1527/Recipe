Chicken fry

Chicken fry is a mouthwatering culinary delight that never fails to tantalize the taste buds. Succulent pieces of chicken, marinated in a flavorful blend of spices and herbs, are coated in a crispy, golden-brown crust through a careful process of breading and deep frying. The result is a harmonious fusion of tender, juicy meat and a tantalizingly crunchy exterior that offers a symphony of flavors and textures with each bite. Whether seasoned with traditional spices or infused with exotic flavors from around the world, chicken fry is a universally beloved dish that embodies the perfect balance of savory and indulgence, making it a timeless favorite among food enthusiasts.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : breakfast food

instructions

step 1

Start by marinating the chicken pieces in a mixture of spices and herbs. Common ingredients include salt, black pepper, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, and optionally, some yogurt or buttermilk for tenderness and flavor. Allow the chicken to soak in the marinade for at least 30 minutes, or ideally, refrigerate it for a few hours to let the flavors penetrate the meat.

step 2

After marinating, coat each chicken piece with a layer of breadcrumbs or a mixture of flour and breadcrumbs. This coating creates the crispy, golden exterior of the chicken fry. Press the breadcrumbs onto the chicken firmly to ensure they adhere well.

step 3

Heat oil in a deep pan or fryer to around 350-375°F (175-190°C). Carefully place the coated chicken pieces into the hot oil, ensuring not to overcrowd the pan. Fry the chicken until it turns a beautiful golden brown and is cooked through, which typically takes around 6-8 minutes depending on the size of the pieces. Use a slotted spoon to remove the fried chicken and place it on a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil. Serve hot, and enjoy your delicious homemade chicken fry!

ingredients

Chicken

oil (for cooking)

Salt and pepper (to taste)

tools

Non-stick skillet

frying pan

Spatula
